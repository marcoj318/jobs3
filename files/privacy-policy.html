When visiting RemoteCareers we and our business partners may use tracking technologies to customize and enhance your online experience. Software tools may be used during some visits to measure and collect session information, such as how long it takes a page to respond, download errors, how long it takes you to read certain pages, and how you interact with those pages. You can also encounter tracking technologies from third parties on certain pages of the websites that we do not control and have not authorized (for example, if you view a web page created by another user, there can be a cookie placed by that web page).

We may use clear GIFs (a.k.a. web beacons, web bugs or pixel tags), in connection with our Site to, among other things, track the activities of Site visitors, help us manage content, and compile statistics about Site usage. Clear GIFs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies. In contrast to cookies, which are stored on your computer’s hard drive, clear GIFs are embedded invisibly on web pages. We and our third-party service providers may also use clear GIFs in HTML emails to our customers, to help us track email response rates, identify when our emails are viewed, and track whether our emails are forwarded.
